Springy weather—Damian Cugley

On Saturday we spotted a modified hoarding on Cowley road that deserved recording, so when we went out for lunch on Sunday 8 February 2004 I took my camera with me. I also took some snaps as we wandered through the Oxford Botanical Garden.
