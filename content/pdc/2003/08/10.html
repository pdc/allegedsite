Percy Street, pages 5–6

10 August 2003

Topics

  • Percy Street

#

I have finished the first two pages of the next chapter of the Percy Street comic strip. If you are wondering why I have not been posting much on this site for the last month, this is the reason (or one reason, CAPTION being another).

Concerned parents might want to check this before allowing delicate young minds to peruse it.

Hope you don’t mind getting the story in two-page chunks. I hope that if I keep it going long enough to actually complete a story then I will learn to draw faster. As it is I am spending an indordinate amount of time ‘pencilling’ each panel, considering how small most of them end up on the sreen...

Update (10 Dec. 2005). Percy Street is now being drawn under a pseudonym Leckford; for updates see Leckford’s LiveJournal.
