Mull 2003

This September Jeremy and Damian visited Damian’s mother Jenny Cugley in her home in Tobermory on the Isle of Mull. It’s a bit of a mixture—forest scenes from walking the dogs (my mother has 5–6 dogs in the house at a time, some of them borrowed from friends), or from visiting Aros Park on our own. There are some close-ups of little stuff as I tested the (super)macro lens on my digital camera.

Update (31 March 2007). This album has been migrated to Flickr to live with my other photos.
