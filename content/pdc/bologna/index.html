Bologna photos - Damian Cugley

The first project meeting of the SEAHORSE 2 project was held in Bologna, in northen Italy, on 22 and 23 October 1998. I took with me the disposable camera that Jeremy gave me to take pictures of Roch Castle. I took some snaps hastily on the free afternoon before the meeting, and some while hurrying to the second day's meeting.
