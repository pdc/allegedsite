8 March 1999 The Scottish village of Aviemore is best known as a skiing resort. My friend Alex’s parents have a time-share on a lodge there which this year they were not using, so Jeremy, Alex, Adrian and I (Damian) headed up to Scotland for a week spent walking in the forest around the nearby Loch an Eilein and Loch Morlich.

Update (2 April 2007) I have migrated this album to Flickr. The Flickr version also uses 100-dpi rather than 75-dpi scans of the original paper photographs.
