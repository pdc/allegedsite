The Three of Wands
The Three of Swords
The Three of Coins

Upright: growth, birth, marriange

Reversed: selfishness, exploitation, domestic problems

The number 3 represents creativity and creation, and combined with the watery, emotional suit of cups, this means a birth, literal or figurative (as in emotional or creative growth). Three is a lucky number, and so this card also suggests a happy family life and good fourtune in matters of the heart.

My glowing baby stick-figure is supposed to evoke Keith Haring’s glowing baby pictures.
