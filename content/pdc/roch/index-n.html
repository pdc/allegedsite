Roch Castle - Damian Cugley

Fifteen of us hired a Roch Castle in west Wales for a week in late October 1998. I had to leave early to go to a business meeting in Bologna in northern Italy, so on my last day there Jeremy gave me a disposable camera to take a few snaps of the outside of the castle.
